Embark on an explosive adventure and conquer cube-based puzzles!

Perfect Blast takes you on a thrilling journey filled with cube-based puzzles and explosive blasts. Dive into a vibrant world of matching colorful cubes and strategically triggering cascading explosions. With ingenious boosters and classic power-ups, you'll clear your path to victory in style.

Join quirky characters like Rusty the Cat, Coal the Owl, and Ollie Frog as they guide you through whimsical cartoon worlds, featuring unique mechanics such as rescuing adorable owls, collecting precious gems, and brewing magical potions.

With over 2100 levels and 20+ engaging mechanics, Perfect Blast guarantees endless hours of fun and challenges.

About us

  • Perfect Blast is a thrilling puzzle game developed by Outlane, Inc. It blends strategy, creativity, and vibrant visuals to deliver an unmatched puzzle-solving experience.
  • Audience Appeal: Designed for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages who enjoy strategic challenges and colorful gameplay.
  • Mission: To provide an engaging and rewarding gameplay experience with endless puzzles and adventures.
  • Gameplay: Match cubes, unlock boosters, and complete levels with unique objectives like saving owls and collecting diamonds.

  • Energetic and Uplifting Soundtrack:
